Matrix Setup Guide

 

Why Use Matrix

Matrix is prvacy focused and usable across many different clients, and is connected to our discord server

Matrix has a lot of options for clients and there is no right "one" to use.

For this guide, we recommend using Element, a simple and easy to use client.

Like Revolt, Matrix is also not blocked on the School Board's Wifi, meaning you can still contact and chat with your friends without and problems

Getting Started

Account making

Head to Matrix's Website and follow their instructions on making an account, afterward, go to Elements Website, or take a look at some of the clients showcased on Matrix's Website

Picking a client & Logging in

After you've picked the client your using, log in with your matrix account, then hit this link to join our server

Verifying and Finishing up

After that, you'll need to verify yourself with our verifier, to confirm that you're from OCDSB. After that you'll have an easy and secure way to chat with the Colonel By Community
