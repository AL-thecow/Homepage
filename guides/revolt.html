Revolt Chat Setup Guide

 

Why Use Revolt

Revolt is a lot like discord, in looks and in features. However, Revolt allows for more customization by the user, and nothing is locked behind a paywall.

Another bonus of Revolt is that it isn't blocked on the school's wifi, meaning that you could still message your friends when needed at school.

Revolt is also bridged to a few channels of our discord server. So you'll still be connected with those not on our Revolt server!

Getting Started

Making an account

Like discord, you'll need to head to Revolts Website to make an account, once you do, you'll be greeted by the browser version of their app.

Joining the server

Click this link to join our server, in a bit, we'll contact you to verify you ourselves, our point you to our verifier

Finishing Up

You're now all done! you can change your profile picture and banner, and start chatting with your friends!
